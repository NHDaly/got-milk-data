🍼 Baby Feed Rate Analyzer

Average milk consumption rate (ml/hour), sampled every 15 minutes. Each feed is weighted by a window centered on the sample time. The window duration sets how many hours each average spans; the smoothing morphs that window from a hard box (0%) to a full cosine bell (100%).
